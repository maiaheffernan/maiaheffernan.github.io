# Comparing miniDOT oxygen readings and the Concerto CTDO casts in June 2026

The miniDOTs in the shellfish rafts are not reading well so I plotted the CTD casts at each mooring location and at the shellfish rafts. The miniDOT DO readings are not at the exact same time, but I pulled the closest time stamp. Also, I am not 100% sure about the miniDOT depths, but I made the assumption that the moorings were at a 45 degree angle and calculated the miniDOT depths based on the as-deployed line depths.


Also, I double-checked Emily's previous email and they had their sensors hanging **in** the shellfish rafts, not outside them. It looks like they were in a really similar spot to where we have them now.

One thing to note is that the raft miniDOT sensor data is inside the rafts and the cast data is outside the rafts. I am thinking the miniDOTs are not great readings, but this is just another confounding variable. The miniDOT spec sheet says that the readings are accurate to 0.3 mg/L. All the miniDOT reading inaccuracies are outside this spec value from the Concerto data.
